Bella Ciao

The song used several times in the series,
"Bella Ciao", is partisan hymn, adapted from
an old Italian country fork song from the
later part of the 19th century.During ww2,
it represented the Italian resistance against
Benito Mussolini. It is used worldwide as an
anti-fascist hymn of freedom and survival
in hard times.

The Dali Mask

The masks of the robbery are of the famous
Spanish surrealist painter Salvador Dali, who
spoke highly of causing confusion and not
limiting one's imagination exactly what the
professor and the heist gang are doing.
